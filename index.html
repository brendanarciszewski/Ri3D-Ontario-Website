ABOUT Ri3D
Ri3D (Robot in 3 Days) is an awesome, community-driven experience, and an unofficial part of the FIRST Robotics Competition. (View Intro to Ri3D Ontario PDF) Ri3D 2018 will start January 6, 2018, at 10:30am, and end 72 hours later. Stay tuned regarding resources that will be available to participants. Entering its first year in Ontario, Ri3D will be an exciting way for roboticists to inspire the next generation of thinkers. To apply and see the requirements, complete our form.
Application Deadline: Saturday, November 25th, 2017 at 11:59pm
Have questions? Email us at Ri3DOntario@gmail.com
VALUES

We want to make FRC more competitive in Ontario. Here are what guides us.

Openness

We want everyone to have the chance to participate in Ri3D. As such, all activities undertaken by this team will be publicized. There will be a livestream and active social media engagement throughout Ri3D.

Quality through Innovation

Our main objective is to inspire other teams to build better robots. As such, we will rigorously design and prototype to develop the best robot possible in 72 hours.

Safety

To ensure that everyone has fun at Ri3D, we need to keep safety as a top priority. As such, we will follow all rules and regulations of the building. We will require that you show us your ability to use machining tools, and only once this has been shown will you be allowed to use equipment. There may be forms you will have to sign. We may ask you to provide character references.

Robodrome

The HWCDSB Robodrome is a space in Hamilton, Ontario for teams to practice and work. This space features a nearly full-size practice field as well as a shop, and is currently housing teams 4618 & 5406. They'll be graciously hosting us for the duration of our build period.
